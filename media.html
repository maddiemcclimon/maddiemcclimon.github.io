Best Oat Content Creators

Oats are viral on social media! On instagram, tons of oat chefs post their daily bowls. On TikTok (or "OatTok") you can follow oat experts for outstanding recipies and tips. Trace and Min are two creators that are especailly good at making baked oats, where they blend all of the indredients so the finished product looks more like a cake.

Trace Alexander @traceoats

Trace Alexander is one of the most popular oat content creators on the internet. Trace has 1.1 million followers and nearly 50 million likes on TikTok.
